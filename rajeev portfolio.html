Who I Am

I am an Identity and Access Management Engineer with over 10 years of experience across Indian enterprises and MNCs. My work sits at the intersection of identity security, cloud infrastructure, and compliance — making sure the right people have the right access, and nothing more.

Currently at LTIMindtree, I work on complex AD migration and decommissioning projects, Microsoft Entra ID deployments, and enterprise IAM governance programs. Previously at TCS and Capgemini.

Outside of client work, I write about IAM careers for Indian IT professionals at Identity Decoded — because this is one of the most in-demand specialisations in Indian IT right now, and almost nobody explains it clearly.
